# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 2026**

## 1. Data We Collect

### Photos
- The photos you submit for puzzle creation.
- AI-generated intermediate and final images.

### Order Information
- Etsy order ID and shipping details (handled by Etsy).
- Style preferences and subject descriptions you provide.

### Technical Data
- Consent logs (timestamp, order ID, consent method).
- Quality scores and processing metadata (internal use only).

## 2. How We Use Your Data

- **Photo processing:** Your photos are sent to AI processing services to create the fantasy character artwork.
- **Quality assurance:** We run automated quality checks (face similarity, image quality) to ensure the output meets standards.
- **Order fulfillment:** We use your order details to produce and deliver your puzzle.

## 3. Third-Party Processing

Your photos are processed through:
- **Replicate** (replicate.com) - AI model hosting platform. Photos are sent to their API for processing. See Replicate's privacy policy at replicate.com/privacy.
- **Print supplier** (createjigsawpuzzles.com or similar) - The final print-ready image is sent to our print supplier for physical puzzle production.

We do not sell or share your photos with any other third parties.

## 4. Data Storage & Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period | Storage |
|-----------|-----------------|---------|
| Customer photos | 30 days after order completion | Encrypted local storage |
| AI intermediates | 30 days after order completion | Local storage |
| Final puzzle design | Duration of business records | Local storage |
| Consent logs | 3 years (legal requirement) | Local storage |
| Order metadata | Duration of business records | Local storage |

## 5. Your Rights

### Access
You can request a copy of all data we hold about you by contacting us.

### Deletion
You can request deletion of your photos and personal data at any time. We will delete within 7 business days, except where retention is legally required.

### Rectification
You can request correction of any inaccurate data.

### Data Portability
You can request your data in a machine-readable format.

## 6. GDPR (EU Customers)

If you are in the EU/EEA:
- Legal basis for processing: Performance of a contract (fulfilling your order) and legitimate interest (quality assurance).
- You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
- Data transfers outside the EU are covered by standard contractual clauses.

## 7. CCPA (California Customers)

If you are a California resident:
- We do not sell your personal information.
- You have the right to know what data we collect and to request deletion.
- You will not be discriminated against for exercising your rights.

## 8. Security

- Photos are stored on encrypted storage during processing.
- API communications use HTTPS encryption.
- Access to customer data is limited to the business operator.

## 9. Children's Privacy

- We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
- Photos of minors may only be submitted by their parent or legal guardian.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated through our Etsy shop.

## Contact

For privacy-related questions or data requests, contact us through our Etsy shop messaging.
