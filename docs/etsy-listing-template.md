Custom AI Fantasy Character Puzzle - Personalized Jigsaw Puzzle Gift from Your Photo | 500 or 1000 Pieces

## Description

**Turn your loved one into a magical character!**

Send us a photo and we'll transform it into a stunning fantasy character placed in a magical world - then print it as a high-quality jigsaw puzzle. Perfect for birthdays, holidays, and "just because" gifts!

**How It Works:**
1. Choose your puzzle size (500 or 1000 pieces)
2. Select your style (see options below)
3. Place your order and send us a clear photo via Etsy message
4. We'll create a digital preview for your approval within 2-3 business days
5. Once approved, your puzzle ships within 5-7 business days

**Available Styles:**
- Fairytale - Enchanted prince/princess in a magical forest with glowing castles
- Superhero - Comic-book guardian atop a futuristic skyline
- Storybook Cartoon - Pixar-like character in a whimsical village
- Pixel Quest - Retro 8-bit RPG adventurer in a fantasy world

**Puzzle Details:**
- 500 pieces: ~16" x 20" completed
- 1000 pieces: ~20" x 28" completed
- Premium cardboard, vibrant colors
- Comes in a sturdy box

**Tips for Best Results:**
- Send a clear, well-lit photo
- Face should be clearly visible
- One person per photo works best
- Avoid heavy filters or sunglasses

**Important Notes:**
- By submitting a photo, you confirm you have the right to use it and consent to AI processing
- We preview every design before printing - you approve before we print!
- Photos are deleted within 30 days of order completion
- See our Privacy Policy for details

**Processing Time:** 5-7 business days after preview approval
**Shipping:** Standard shipping included (US). International available.
